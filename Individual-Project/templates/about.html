THE GELATO OF LAGGENDA

With all due respect to ice cream (and there is respect!) there is nothing like gelato. What is the difference? You will be surprised, but the difference is... in the air. In the preparation of traditional Italian gelato, the high-quality ingredients are mixed slowly and carefully. Such mixing allows only a small amount of air to enter the freezer (about 20-30% air compared to 3 times in normal ice cream) and thus a rich, deep and full flavor is created. Want more differences between gelato and ice cream? you are welcome! While normal, industrial ice cream is produced in the amount of thousands of liters per flavor in order to optimize and save on production costs, real gelato is made in small batches. Here, at Legenda, we prepare only about 3-4 kg of each flavor each time so that you get it in its perfect texture every time.
